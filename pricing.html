FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we get asked most.

What file formats can I export my video in?
Export format depends on your plan. The Free tier outputs MP4 (H.264, 720p). Pro adds WebM (VP9) and GIF support at up to 1080p. The Studio plan unlocks everything: MP4, WebM, GIF, and APNG — all up to 4K. Remotion handles the server-side render, so quality is frame-perfect regardless of format.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes — cancel anytime from your account dashboard with one click. No cancellation fees, no minimum terms. When you cancel, your plan stays active until the end of the billing period. After that, your account rolls back to the Free tier automatically and you keep access to your previously exported files.
Does Mojin have an API for programmatic rendering?
The REST API is available on the Studio plan. It lets you trigger renders, pass template parameters, poll job status, and download outputs — all without touching the browser editor. Ideal for automated pipelines, CMS integrations, or batch video generation. API keys are managed in your Studio dashboard. Full API docs are coming soon; Studio subscribers get early access.
How do team seats work in the Studio plan?
The Studio plan includes 5 team seats by default — each seat gets full Pro-level access plus shared template libraries and a unified export quota. You can add extra seats for €8 per seat per month. All seats share the Studio workspace, so your team can collaborate on the same project assets without duplicating work.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not happy within the first 7 days after upgrading, contact us at support@mojin.app and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked. For annual plans, a pro-rated refund is available within 30 days of purchase.
